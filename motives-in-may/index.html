Motives in May

Organized by Jake Huryn and William C. Newman.

The goal of this minicourse is to learn something about Voevodsky's triangulated category of mixed motives, with the hope of seeing some ideas from the proof of the norm residue isomorphism theorem.

References:
[MVW] Mazza–Voevodsky–Weibel, Lecture Notes on Motivic Cohomology
[HW]  Haesemeyer–Weibel, The Norm Residue Theorem in Motivic Cohomology
